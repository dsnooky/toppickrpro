How Freelancing Works

Freelancing allows individuals to offer services to clients without being employed full-time by one company.

Popular Freelancing Platforms

Websites such as Upwork, Fiverr, and Freelancer connect freelancers with clients worldwide.

Skills and Portfolio

Freelancers showcase their skills through portfolios, samples, and experience.

Setting Realistic Expectations

Freelancing income varies. Beginners often start small and grow over time.

Conclusion

Freelancing is a flexible and legitimate way to earn online with consistent effort.
